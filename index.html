Term 1, Week 2

Creating Cel-Based Animation and GIF

Term 1, Week 4

For one of my IST projects, I created a short cel-based animation using Google Slides and made a simple GIF of a bouncing ball. It was a fun introduction to animation!

A cel-based animation is created by drawing individual frames, with each slide in Google Slides acting as a new frame. I used this technique to animate a character or object, making it appear to move. I also made a GIF of a bouncing ball, which helped me understand looping and frame rates in animation. It was a cool way to explore motion graphics without using complex software.
